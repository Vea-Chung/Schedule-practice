How to Use OptimaSchedule

  1. 1
    Download the Template

    Click "Download Excel Template" from the sidebar to get a configured layout. This ensures your inputs conform exactly to the solver's requirements.

  2. 2
    Fill in Your Data

    Open the spreadsheet and complete the sheets: Setup, Resources, Departments, and Capability Mappings. Do not rename columns or sheets.

  3. 3
    Upload & Configure

    Drag your completed spreadsheet into the upload area. Adjust the Start and End dates in the sidebar to match your planning horizon.

  4. 4
    Generate & Roster

    Click "Generate Schedule". The engine calculates optimal assignments client-side, showing alternative options (if any) or highlighting violations/reasons if no perfect fit exists.
